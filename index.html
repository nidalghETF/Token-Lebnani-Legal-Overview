Executive Summary

Token-Lebnani is a regulated real estate tokenization platform that unlocks Lebanon's $200+ billion real estate market for global investors. Our innovative legal structure combines Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) regulatory oversight with Lebanese property assets, eliminating traditional barriers while providing unprecedented security and liquidity.

The platform addresses three critical pain points: (1) High capital requirements ($500K+ minimum for traditional Lebanese property investment), (2) Banking system exposure and capital controls, and (3) Illiquid assets with no exit strategy. Through fractional tokenized ownership starting at $1,000, investors gain exposure to Lebanon's most stable asset class with complete banking independence.

Our hybrid legal structure—ADGM Foundation Companies owning Lebanese S.A.R.L. SPVs—provides regulatory credibility, tax mitigation (from 6% to ~0.25%), and automated income distribution via smart contracts. With projected revenues of $3M in Year 1 scaling to $25M by Year 3, Token-Lebnani represents a transformative opportunity in Middle Eastern real estate investment.
