Legal Innovation Framework

Sophisticated cross-border structure ensuring regulatory compliance, investor protection, and operational efficiency.

Our legal framework represents a breakthrough in cross-border asset tokenization, expertly navigating the complexities of ADGM and Lebanese jurisdictions. The structure begins with an ADGM Foundation Company—a non-profit entity established in Abu Dhabi Global Market that serves as the sole shareholder of all Lebanese SPVs. This foundation acts as a bare trustee for token holders, providing stable ownership while ensuring that beneficial ownership rights flow directly to investors.

Each property is held by an individual Lebanese S.A.R.L. (Société à Responsabilité Limitée), creating clean legal separation and isolating risk. The Tripartite Agreement—a legally binding document between the ADGM Foundation, Lebanese SPV, and Token-Lebnani platform—creates an enforceable governance bridge. This agreement ensures the foundation votes SPV shares according to token holder instructions, with TLB providing the voting platform and communicating results through a legally binding waterfall for income distribution.

The governing law split is deliberate and strategic: ADGM law governs the platform and tokens, while Lebanese law governs property and SPV matters. This ensures appropriate jurisdiction for each component while providing clear dispute resolution pathways. All token holders undergo rigorous KYC/AML verification, and the ERC-1404 token standard includes built-in transfer restrictions to maintain regulatory compliance.
