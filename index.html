Legal Innovation Framework

Our legal framework represents a breakthrough in cross-border asset tokenization, expertly navigating the complexities of ADGM and Lebanese jurisdictions. The structure begins with an ADGM Foundation Company—a non-profit entity established in Abu Dhabi Global Market that serves as the sole shareholder of all Lebanese SPVs.

Each property is held by an individual Lebanese S.A.R.L., creating clean legal separation and isolating risk. The Tripartite Agreement creates an enforceable governance bridge, ensuring the foundation votes SPV shares according to token holder instructions, with TLB providing the voting platform and communicating results through a legally binding waterfall for income distribution.
